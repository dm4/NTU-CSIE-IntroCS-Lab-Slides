Y2K38 - Spam from Future

Y2K38 - Year 2038 Problem

  • Use a signed 32-bit integer

  • Interpret this number as the number of seconds since 00:00:00 UTC on Thursday, 1 January 1970.

  • Also known as Unix Millennium Bug

1970/1/1 00:00:00 UTC

2,147,483,647 secs

About 24,855 days

About 68 years

Y2K38

  • How it happens?

  • Big Problem!!!

Y2K38 - Solution

  • Use a signed 64-bit integer

  • It won't overflow until Year 292,277,026,596.
